01

Why we launched the ASCAN Training

We believe Web3/NFTs will change the world forever and should be accessible for everyone - and want to make the entry hurdle as small as possible. Web3 is about communities and sharing so we fight for avoiding exclusivity and want to give as many people as possible the oppertunity to take part and thrive.

02

For whom we created it

ASCAN Training is for everyone who thought about getting into NFTs, Crypto or Web3 but didn't do the final step yet for one reason or another. Getting your first NFT will help the user to get a foot in the door and from there is free to discover all the possibilities the space has to offer. We are certain everyone can find his place here and is welcomed with open arms.

03

How we will do it

Our beautifully designed, interactive learning course guides the user safely through every step nessecary to access the world of NFTs. After completing the course the user will get a free NFT as a reward, a beautiful memory of his first web3 experience which also functions as an entry token for our ASCAN Academy where we will teach some more in depth topics which will help the user navigate and discover the space.

Discover Web3

  1. Possibilitys

    Own your AssetsYes, you really OWN your assets. If you own an nft it is only yours and it can't be taken away from you.

  2. Security

    If stored properly NFTs are as secure as it gets and can't get stolen or lost. With a safe wallet setup you are at no risk of losing your collected assets.

  3. Membership

    NFTs can be used to verify memberships for various scenarios. For example communities, events (tickets) and for services.

  4. Accessibility

    Taking artworks as an example, instead of only viewable and tradable in galleries, they can be accessed and displayed from everywhere in the world.

  5. Transparency and Authenticity

    With the Blockchain technology NFT transactions are effortlessly trackable. Ensuring authenticity, creatorship and ownership of the Asset.

  6. Investments and Royalties

    Because NFT is a technology with room to grow, it can be a valuable investment. Copyright royalties allow for a long-term income stream for creators, such as artists and many others.
